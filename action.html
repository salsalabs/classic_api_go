Format notes

  • Salsa timestamps are encoded as "YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS", where all elements are numeric and "HH" is on a 24 hour clock.
  • Salsa timestamps are in the "America/NewYork" timezone (GMT-5), aka Eastern Standard Time (EST).
